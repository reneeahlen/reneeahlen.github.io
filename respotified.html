Rebranding Assignment

Choose a business to rebrand and research information. Organize a clear company profile, designing a layout that is graphically interesting and informative. Combine your design to create a business card, envelope and letterhead using the design choices to create a cohesive stationery system.

Create a brochure and postcard that connects your identity pieces together with your company by using graphic elements and compatible designs. Create a Press Kit for your company with a cohesive design that connects all your current identity pieces together. A Press Kit is designed to hold marketing materials and can be viewed as a pre-packaged folder.
