ABOUT ME

My name is Renee Aahlen, I moved from northern Sweden to southern California to become a graphic design student and with the goal to graduate with a degree from Santa Monica College after Spring semester 2021. When I graduated from my high school back in 2008 I had no idea if I wanted to continue studying or what I wanted to work with. This resulted in that I took some part time jobs with different directions. Some of them include; working with young adults at a youth recreation center, managing a bar at our local ice hockey games and worked at different fast food restaurants until I found myself a position at my hometowns public library. Working at a public library reopened my eyes for culture, art and communication.
